Overview

My Amusement Park is a time management and tycoon mobile game for iOS and Android where players design, build, and operate their own amusement park empire. This published product challenges players to strategically place attractions, manage resources, and keep visitors happy while expanding their park into the ultimate entertainment destination.

Key Features

Park Building & Customization

Design your dream park with unique rides and attractions. Customize layouts and themes to reflect your vision.

Visitor Management

Provide food, restrooms and entertainment to keep visitors happy. Satisfied guests spend more and recommend your park.

Economic Simulation

Balance income against maintenance costs and staff salaries. Make strategic pricing and investment decisions for success.

Staff Management

Hire staff like operators, janitors and entertainers. Assign them strategically to maximize efficiency and satisfaction.

Design Challenge

Early testing showed that 29% of players were leaving after unlocking the 2nd ride of the park, creating a significant retention problem in the critical early gameplay phase.

What I Changed

  • Improved the onboarding flow by properly guiding the user without making the user feel restricted
  • Added points of interest for the user to go and do more actions
  • Improved reward spacing on the user onboarding

Outcome

The improved onboarding experience resulted in improved retention D1 from 29% to 34% and improved churn on ride #2 by 23%. These metrics demonstrated that the revised approach successfully addressed the critical early-game retention problem.

What I Learned

The importance of having a rewarding and directed onboarding, but also giving the player the freedom to do his own choices
