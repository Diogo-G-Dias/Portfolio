About the Game

My Amusement Park is an engaging time management and tycoon game where players design, build, and operate their own amusement park empire. From humble beginnings with a single attraction, players expand their park into a thriving entertainment destination that attracts visitors from around the world.

The game combines strategic planning with fast-paced time management gameplay, challenging players to balance customer satisfaction, park expansion, and financial management. With a variety of rides, attractions, and amenities to build, each playthrough offers a unique park development experience.

Key Features

Park Building & Customization

Design and build your dream amusement park with dozens of unique rides, attractions, and amenities. Customize layouts, themes, and decorations to create a park that reflects your vision.

Visitor Management

Manage visitor needs and expectations by providing food, restrooms, entertainment, and efficient pathways. Happy visitors spend more money and recommend your park to others.

Economic Simulation

Balance income from ticket sales and in-park purchases against maintenance costs, staff salaries, and expansion expenses. Strategic pricing and investment decisions are key to success.

Staff Management

Hire and train staff members including ride operators, janitors, entertainers, and security. Assign them to optimal locations to maximize park efficiency and visitor satisfaction.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Complex Economic Balancing

Creating a balanced economy that was challenging but not frustrating for players across different skill levels proved difficult.

Solution:

We developed a dynamic economic model that subtly adjusts to player behavior, providing appropriate challenges without becoming impossible. This included implementing multiple income streams and strategic expense management options.

Challenge: Mobile Performance with Large Parks

Maintaining smooth performance on mobile devices when parks grew to include numerous attractions and visitors was technically demanding.

Solution:

We implemented an optimized rendering system in Unity that dynamically adjusts detail levels based on device capabilities. Additionally, we created efficient visitor AI that uses pooling techniques to minimize performance impact.

Challenge: Intuitive Building Controls

Creating an intuitive building system that worked well on small mobile screens presented significant UX challenges.

Solution:

We designed a streamlined building interface with smart placement assistance and gesture controls that made construction intuitive even on smaller devices. The system includes helpful guides and visual feedback to assist players.
