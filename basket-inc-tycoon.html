About the Game

Basket Inc. Tycoon is an addictive idle game that puts players in charge of their own basketball court with the goal of becoming the richest basketball manager alive. Starting with a basic court and minimal resources, players gradually build a basketball empire through strategic upgrades and investments.

The game combines the satisfying progression mechanics of idle games with the excitement of basketball management. Players can check in for a few minutes to collect earnings and make upgrades, or actively play to maximize their income through strategic decisions and mini-games.

Key Features

Court Management

Design Challenge

Initial playtests showed that players were getting stuck in the mid-game progression, with 42% of users abandoning the game after reaching the second court upgrade due to perceived slow progression.

What I Changed

  • Redesigned the upgrade curve to provide more frequent small rewards
  • Added mini-achievements to create short-term goals
  • Implemented a dynamic court environment that visually evolves with player progress

Outcome

The improved progression system resulted in increased retention at the mid-game point by 37% and improved overall session length by 24%. These metrics demonstrated that the revised approach successfully addressed the mid-game progression problem.

What I Learned

The importance of providing frequent small rewards and visual progression feedback to maintain player engagement
