About the Game

Basket Inc. Tycoon is an addictive idle game that puts players in charge of their own basketball court with the goal of becoming the richest basketball manager alive. Starting with a basic court and minimal resources, players gradually build a basketball empire through strategic upgrades and investments.

The game combines the satisfying progression mechanics of idle games with the excitement of basketball management. Players can check in for a few minutes to collect earnings and make upgrades, or actively play to maximize their income through strategic decisions and mini-games.

Key Features

Court Management

Upgrade your basketball court with better equipment, facilities, and amenities to attract more players and increase revenue. Each upgrade visually transforms your court, creating a satisfying progression.

Player & Mascot System

Recruit and upgrade basketball players and mascots with unique abilities that boost your income. Each character has distinct skills and personality traits that affect your basketball operation.

Income Boosters

Utilize various income boosters and multipliers to accelerate your earnings. Strategic activation of these boosters during key moments can dramatically increase your profits.

Achievements & Challenges

Complete achievements and daily challenges to earn special rewards and bonuses. The achievement system provides additional goals beyond the main progression path.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Balancing Idle Progression

Creating a progression system that felt rewarding both for active players and those who check in occasionally presented a significant balancing challenge.

Solution:

We developed a multi-tiered progression system with both short and long-term goals. Active players can optimize their strategies and engage with mini-games, while casual players benefit from offline earnings and simplified upgrade paths.

Challenge: Visual Feedback for Idle Mechanics

Making an idle game visually engaging and providing satisfying feedback for player actions when most progress happens automatically was difficult.

Solution:

We created a dynamic court environment that visually evolves with player progress, along with animated income indicators and celebration effects for milestones. The visual progression helps players feel a sense of accomplishment.

Challenge: Monetization Balance

Implementing monetization that enhanced the player experience without creating frustrating paywalls required careful design.

Solution:

We focused on optional boosters and cosmetic upgrades that added value without being required for progression. Additionally, we implemented a generous reward system for regular players, ensuring the game remained enjoyable for non-paying users.
