About the Game

Limited Survive is an action-packed survival game that throws players into a post-apocalyptic world filled with hordes of undead enemies. Part of the popular "Limited" series from Kool Games, this thrilling browser-based experience challenges players to stay alive as long as possible while battling against increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

The game combines strategic decision-making with fast-paced action, requiring players to carefully manage resources, select powerful upgrades, and navigate dangerous environments. With its accessible controls (WASD, arrow keys, or mouse drag for movement) and intuitive upgrade system, Limited Survive delivers an intense gaming experience that's both challenging and rewarding.

Key Features

Versatile Controls

Play your way with multiple control options - WASD keys, arrow keys, or click and drag with the mouse for intuitive movement across dangerous terrain.

Strategic Upgrades

Select from various powerful upgrades throughout your run, creating unique build combinations that dramatically change your survival strategy.

Undead Hordes

Battle against waves of increasingly challenging enemies in a post-apocalyptic setting, with each wave bringing new threats and challenges.

Cross-Platform Play

Enjoy seamless gameplay across mobile devices and desktop computers, with optimized performance for all web browsers and screen sizes.

  • Developing the upgrade selection system that allows players to choose different abilities
  • Implementing the multi-control scheme (keyboard and mouse options)
  • Balancing enemy difficulty scaling to create an engaging challenge curve

  • Creating engaging visual feedback systems for player actions and enemy encounters

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Undead Enemy AI

Creating enemy AI that was challenging but not overwhelming for players across different skill levels proved difficult in a browser environment.

Solution:

We developed a tiered enemy behavior system that gradually introduces more complex attack patterns as players progress, with enemy difficulty scaling based on survival time rather than fixed waves.

Challenge: Upgrade Balance

Ensuring all upgrade paths were viable without creating overpowered combinations required extensive testing.

Solution:

We implemented a dynamic upgrade offering system that analyzes player's current build and presents balanced options, preventing game-breaking combinations while still allowing for strategic depth.

Challenge: Mobile Performance

Maintaining smooth performance on mobile devices while rendering numerous enemies and effects was technically demanding.

Solution:

We created an adaptive rendering system in Construct 3 that dynamically adjusts visual effects and enemy count based on device performance, ensuring consistent gameplay across all platforms.
