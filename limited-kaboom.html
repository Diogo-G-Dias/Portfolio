About the Game

Limited Kaboom is the ultimate physics-based destruction game that puts explosive action at your fingertips. Part of the popular "Limited" series from Kool Games, this thrilling browser-based experience challenges players to strategically place and detonate bombs to overcome obstacles and defeat enemies.

The game combines tactical decision-making with satisfying physics destruction, requiring players to carefully aim and time their explosions to maximize effectiveness. With its simple drag-and-release controls and progressive difficulty, Limited Kaboom delivers an engaging gaming experience that's easy to learn but challenging to master.

Design Challenge

Initial testing showed that 38% of players were finding the game too difficult to progress past level 15, with many citing frustration with the precision required for successful bomb placement.

What I Changed

  • Implemented a more forgiving blast radius system for explosions
  • Added a trajectory prediction line to help with aiming
  • Redesigned early levels to better teach core mechanics

Outcome

The improved control system and level design resulted in increased player progression through level 15 by 45% and improved overall session length by 28%. Player feedback became significantly more positive, with many praising the "satisfying yet accessible" gameplay.

What I Learned

The importance of providing clear visual feedback for precision mechanics and balancing challenge with accessibility
