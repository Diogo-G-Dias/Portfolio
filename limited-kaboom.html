About the Game

Limited Kaboom is the ultimate physics-based destruction game that puts explosive action at your fingertips. Part of the popular "Limited" series from Kool Games, this thrilling browser-based experience challenges players to strategically place and detonate bombs to overcome obstacles and defeat enemies.

The game combines tactical decision-making with satisfying physics destruction, requiring players to carefully aim and time their explosions to maximize effectiveness. With its simple drag-and-release controls and progressive difficulty, Limited Kaboom delivers an engaging gaming experience that's easy to learn but challenging to master.

Key Features

Physics-Based Destruction

Experience realistic physics as structures collapse and enemies fly through the air after perfectly placed explosions.

Intuitive Controls

Simple drag-and-release mechanics make the game accessible to players of all skill levels while still offering depth.

Strategic Challenges

Each level presents unique puzzles and enemy configurations that require creative thinking to overcome.

Cross-Platform Play

Enjoy seamless gameplay across mobile devices and desktop computers, with optimized performance for all web browsers and screen sizes.

My Contribution

As a key member of the development team for Limited Kaboom at Kool Games, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the core physics-based destruction mechanics
  • Developing the intuitive drag-and-release control system
  • Creating engaging level designs with progressive difficulty
  • Implementing realistic physics interactions between bombs, structures, and enemies

  • Collaborating with the team to ensure the game maintained the "Limited" series feel while offering unique gameplay

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Physics Performance

Implementing realistic physics for destruction while maintaining smooth performance across all devices was technically challenging.

Solution:

We developed an optimized physics system in Construct 3 that scales complexity based on device capabilities, ensuring consistent gameplay without sacrificing the satisfying destruction effects.

Challenge: Difficulty Progression

Creating a balanced difficulty curve that challenged players without becoming frustrating required extensive testing.

Solution:

We implemented a dynamic difficulty system that subtly adjusts based on player performance, providing an appropriate challenge for players of varying skill levels.

Challenge: Intuitive Controls

Designing controls that worked equally well on touchscreens and mouse-based interfaces presented unique design challenges.

Solution:

We created an adaptive control system that automatically detects the user's input method and optimizes the experience accordingly, with special attention to touch precision on mobile devices.
