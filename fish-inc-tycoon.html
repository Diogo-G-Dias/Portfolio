About the Game

Fish Inc Tycoon is an engaging idle simulation game that transforms players from humble single rod fishermen into fishing industry moguls. The game combines relaxing fishing mechanics with strategic business management as players expand their operations from a small dock to a vast ocean empire.

With its satisfying progression system and charming visual style, Fish Inc Tycoon offers both quick play sessions for casual gamers and deeper strategic elements for those who want to optimize their fishing empire. The game's idle mechanics ensure progress continues even when players are away.

Key Features

Fishing Empire Building

Start with a single fishing rod and gradually expand to multiple docks, boats, and advanced fishing equipment. Watch your humble operation transform into a sprawling fishing enterprise with visual progression.

Crew Management

Hire and upgrade fishermen with unique skills and specialties to improve your fishing efficiency. Each crew member contributes differently to your operation, allowing for strategic team building.

Boat & Equipment Upgrades

Invest in better boats, fishing gear, and technology to increase your catch rate and value. Each upgrade visibly enhances your fishing fleet and unlocks new fishing capabilities.

Ocean Exploration

Unlock new fishing zones with rare and valuable fish species as you expand your operation. Different zones offer unique challenges and rewards, encouraging strategic decisions about where to focus your efforts.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Making Fishing Engaging in an Idle Game

Creating fishing mechanics that were satisfying both as an automated idle process and during active play required careful design.

Solution:

We developed a dual-layer fishing system where the base idle mechanics provide consistent progress, while optional active fishing mini-games offer bonus rewards and a more engaging experience for players who want to actively participate.

Challenge: Progression Pacing

Balancing the progression speed to keep players engaged without making the game feel too slow or too quick to complete was challenging.

Solution:

We implemented a dynamic progression system with multiple upgrade paths that players can pursue simultaneously. This creates a sense of constant progress while maintaining long-term goals. Early upgrades provide immediate satisfaction, while later upgrades require more strategic planning.

Challenge: Visual Representation of Scale

Representing the growth from a single fisherman to a massive fishing empire within the constraints of a mobile screen presented significant visual design challenges.

Solution:

We created a zoom-out effect as players progress, gradually revealing more of the ocean and fishing operation. This visual scaling helps convey the growing scope of the player's empire while maintaining clarity on smaller screens. Key elements remain visually distinct regardless of scale.
