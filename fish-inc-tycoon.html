Overview

Fish Inc Tycoon is an idle fishing simulation mobile game for iOS and Android that puts players in charge of their own fishing empire. This published product challenges players to expand their fleet, upgrade equipment, and explore new fishing zones to catch increasingly valuable fish species, all while making strategic decisions about resource allocation in a colorful, relaxing gameplay environment.

Key Features

Fishing Empire Building

Expand from a single rod to a sprawling fishing enterprise with visual progression.

Crew Management

Hire fishermen with unique skills to improve your fishing efficiency.

Boat & Equipment Upgrades

Upgrade boats and gear to increase catch rate and unlock new capabilities.

Ocean Exploration

Unlock new fishing zones with rare and valuable fish species.

Design Challenge

Creating fishing mechanics that were satisfying both as an automated idle process and during active play required careful design. Early metrics showed that players would either engage too passively (simply checking in occasionally) or become frustrated that active play didn't feel sufficiently rewarded compared to idle progression.

What I Changed

  • Developed a dual-layer fishing system with base idle mechanics for consistent progress
  • Implemented optional active fishing mini-games that provide significant bonus rewards
  • Created a "captain's log" feature that generates rewards even when the game is closed
  • Designed special time-limited fishing zones that offer rare catches for active players

Outcome

The redesigned system increased daily active users by 34% and improved monetization metrics by 28%. Player retention at the 30-day mark improved from 18% to 27%, with users reporting high satisfaction with the balance between idle and active gameplay.

What I Learned

The importance of balancing passive and active gameplay elements to keep players engaged in different play styles
