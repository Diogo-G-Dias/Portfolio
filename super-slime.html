About the Game

Super Slime is a fast-paced platformer where you take control of a squishy, bouncy hero on a mission to save the day. With 100 thrilling levels packed with tricky obstacles and fierce enemies, this game challenges players to master the art of stretching, bouncing, and dodging through increasingly difficult environments.

The game combines precision platforming with quick reflexes, requiring players to navigate through challenging terrain while collecting power-ups and avoiding hazards. With its simple controls (WASD or arrow keys) and progressive difficulty, Super Slime delivers an adorable yet challenging adventure that's accessible to players of all skill levels.

Key Features

100 Unique Levels

Journey through 100 meticulously designed levels, each with unique challenges, obstacles, and secrets to discover.

Simple Controls

Easy to learn but hard to master controls using WASD or arrow keys, allowing players to focus on the challenging platforming.

Power-Ups & Abilities

Collect various power-ups that enhance your slime's abilities, allowing for new movement techniques and strategies.

Cross-Platform Play

Enjoy seamless gameplay across mobile devices and desktop computers, with optimized performance for all web browsers and screen sizes.

My Contribution

As a key member of the development team for Super Slime, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the core platforming mechanics and character movement physics
  • Creating engaging level designs with progressive difficulty
  • Implementing the power-up system and special abilities
  • Balancing game difficulty to provide a challenging but fair experience

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Physics-Based Movement

Creating a character that felt both bouncy and precise to control was technically challenging.

Solution:

We developed a custom physics system in Construct 3 that combines predictable movement with dynamic bouncing, giving players precise control while maintaining the slime's squishy character.

Challenge: Level Progression

Designing 100 levels with appropriate difficulty scaling required extensive planning and testing.

Solution:

We created a modular level design system that introduced new mechanics gradually, with each set of levels teaching players new skills before combining them in more complex challenges.

Challenge: Cross-Platform Controls

Ensuring the game controlled well on both desktop and mobile devices presented unique design challenges.

Solution:

We implemented an adaptive control system that automatically detects the user's device and optimizes the experience accordingly, with special attention to touch precision on mobile devices.
