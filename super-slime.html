About the Game

Super Slime is a fast-paced platformer where you take control of a squishy, bouncy hero on a mission to save the day. With 100 thrilling levels packed with tricky obstacles and fierce enemies, this game challenges players to master the art of stretching, bouncing, and dodging through increasingly difficult environments.

The game combines precision platforming with quick reflexes, requiring players to navigate through challenging terrain while collecting power-ups and avoiding hazards. With its simple controls (WASD or arrow keys) and progressive difficulty, Super Slime delivers an adorable yet challenging adventure that's accessible to players of all skill levels.

Design Challenge

Early playtesting revealed that 40% of players were struggling with the difficulty spike between levels 25-30, leading to a significant drop in completion rates for this section of the game.

What I Changed

  • Redesigned the level progression curve to introduce new mechanics more gradually
  • Added optional tutorial elements for more complex jumping techniques
  • Implemented a more forgiving checkpoint system in particularly challenging levels

Outcome

The revised level design resulted in increased completion rates for the problematic section by 35% and improved overall game completion by 22%. Player feedback became significantly more positive, with many praising the "perfect difficulty curve."

What I Learned

Difficulty spikes can be more detrimental than consistent challenge in maintaining player engagement

Optional tutorials allow skilled players to progress quickly while supporting those who need additional guidance
