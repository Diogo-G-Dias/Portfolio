About the Game

Limited Defense is a strategic tower defense game where you command bandits, archers, and explosive towers to hold back relentless waves of enemies. Face off against mischievous Green Slimes, fearsome Orcs, and towering bosses like the Boss King Slime.

The game combines classic tower defense mechanics with a unique resource limitation system, creating a challenging and addictive gameplay experience that tests your strategic thinking and adaptability.

Key Features

Strategic Gameplay

Every decision matters in Limited Defense. Players must carefully choose tower placement and plan upgrades to survive increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

Upgrade System

Upgrade your defenses and unlock legendary warriors with unique abilities to enhance your strategic options and combat effectiveness.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a variety of enemies including Green Slimes, Orcs, and challenging boss encounters like the Boss King Slime that require adaptive strategies.

Tower Variety

Deploy different types of defensive towers including bandits, archers, and explosive towers, each with unique attack patterns and strengths.

My Contribution

As the lead game designer and developer for Limited Defense, I was responsible for:

  • Designing the core gameplay mechanics and tower defense systems
  • Balancing the economy and progression systems
  • Designing the monetization strategy
  • Implementing player retention features and engagement loops
  • Implementing enemy AI and pathing algorithms
  • Creating engaging level designs with increasing difficulty
  • Optimizing performance for smooth gameplay across all web browsers

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Resource Limitation System

Creating a balanced resource system that was challenging but not frustrating required extensive testing and iteration.

Solution:

I implemented a dynamic difficulty scaling system that adjusts resource availability based on player performance, ensuring the game remains challenging but fair for all skill levels.

Challenge: Enemy Variety and Balance

Designing diverse enemies with unique behaviors while maintaining game balance was complex.

Solution:

I created a comprehensive enemy attribute system that allowed for fine-tuning of health, speed, and resistance properties, ensuring each enemy type presented a distinct tactical challenge.
