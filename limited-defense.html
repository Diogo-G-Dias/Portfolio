About the Game

Limited Defense is a strategic tower defense game where you command bandits, archers, and explosive towers to hold back relentless waves of enemies. Face off against mischievous Green Slimes, fearsome Orcs, and towering bosses like the Boss King Slime.

The game combines classic tower defense mechanics with a unique resource limitation system, creating a challenging and addictive gameplay experience that tests your strategic thinking and adaptability.

Key Features

Strategic Gameplay

Carefully choose tower placement and plan upgrades to survive increasingly difficult waves.

Upgrade System

Unlock legendary warriors with unique abilities to enhance your strategic options.

Diverse Enemies

Battle against a variety of enemies from Green Slimes to challenging boss encounters.

Tower Variety

Deploy various defensive towers each with unique attack patterns and strengths.

Design Challenge

Early playtesting revealed that 45% of players were failing at the same point (wave 10) due to a sudden difficulty spike, creating a significant retention problem.

What I Changed

  • Redesigned the wave progression curve to introduce stronger enemies more gradually
  • Added a strategic hint system before challenging waves
  • Implemented a more generous resource allocation in early waves

Outcome

The improved difficulty curve resulted in increased player progression past wave 10 by 52% and improved overall retention by 35%. Player feedback became significantly more positive, with many praising the "strategic depth" and "fair challenge."

What I Learned

Gradual difficulty progression is crucial for player retention in tower defense games

Strategic hints can enhance player learning without diminishing the challenge
